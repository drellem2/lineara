**Reading guide.** A meaningful substrate signal has substrate surfaces ranking *above* the highest-ranked control surface, with a positive Cliff's δ on the per-surface medians and a two-tail Mann-Whitney p < 0.05. If `top_control_rank` is small (e.g. 1-5) the substrate leaderboard is barely separated from the control. If the substrate and control distributions of per-surface medians overlap, the substrate signal at this aggregation level is plausibly artifactual.
