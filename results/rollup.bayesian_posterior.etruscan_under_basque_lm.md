## Notes

- Prior: Beta(α=1, β=1) (uninformative). Posterior mean = (1+k)/(2+n); 95% credible interval from the Beta inverse-CDF (stdlib bisection on the regularized incomplete beta).
- Credibility cap n_min = 10. Surfaces with n < n_min are shrunk toward the prior mean 0.5 in the leaderboard ranking but not excluded; the gate uses posterior_mean directly so small-n outliers can still appear in the top-20 if their raw posterior is high.
- v8 single-root substrate: surface = `pool_entry_surface`; v9 multi-root substrate: surface = each unique entry in `root_surfaces`. A signature like `lur+lur+ur` contributes one observation under `lur` and one under `ur` per window. The control side mirrors this on the matched control pool's surfaces.
- Determinism: identical n + k + posterior + p-values across re-runs given the same `experiments.external_phoneme_perplexity_v0.jsonl`, `hypotheses/auto/*`, `hypotheses/auto_signatures/*`, and `pools/*`. No RNG anywhere in the pipeline.
