## Notes

- A surface counts toward an inscription's `raw_count` once regardless of how many positive records it has on that inscription; the parenthesized number after each surface in `top_substrate_surfaces_present` is the total count of positive paired_diff records over all (v8, v9) records pinning that surface to that inscription.

- A v9 signature like `lur+lur+ur` covering one window contributes one observation under each unique constituent surface (`lur`, `ur`) for that inscription, not two for `lur`.

- `density` is the per-record fraction of substrate records targeting the inscription that contributed at least one top-20 surface with positive evidence; `raw_count` is the *distinct-surface* count and does not double-count the same surface.

- Determinism: identical output across re-runs given the same result stream and manifests. No RNG.
