## Notes

- Constituent surfaces are taken as-listed in the manifest's `root_surfaces`; a signature like `aitz+aitz+oin` is included if all of `aitz` and `oin` are in v10's top-20. Repeats are preserved in the rendered surface list (so the reader sees the actual structure of the signature).

- `paired_diff` is the substrate signature's score minus its matched control's score under the same-LM external phoneme perplexity model (positive = substrate wins).

- Determinism: identical output across re-runs given the same result stream and manifests. No RNG.
