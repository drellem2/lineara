## Notes

- Records: substrate signatures with a matched control: **4832** total across pools.
- Aggregation: per-(pool, sorted root-surface tuple) median over the per-window paired_diff. Surface-sets that appear at multiple windows contribute multiple paired_diff entries; the median over those is the surface-set's row in the per-pool table.
- Acceptance gate (mg-bef2): for at least one pool, Wilcoxon signed-rank one-tail p < 0.05 on per-surface-set medians AND mean per-surface-set median > 0.
