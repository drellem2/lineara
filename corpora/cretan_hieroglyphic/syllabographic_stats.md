# CHIC syllabographic-only corpus (chic-v3, mg-9700)

## Comparison with Linear A

Linear A's `corpus/all.jsonl` carries ~5,000 syllabogram tokens across ~760 inscriptions (mg-99df, mg-d5ef). CHIC's syllabographic-only stream is roughly an order of magnitude smaller. Per the chic-v3 brief: lower statistical power for the right-tail bayesian gate is expected; per-pool p-values should be interpreted with this corpus-size caveat in mind.
